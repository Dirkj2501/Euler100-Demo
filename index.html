What is Euler100?

Not an Olympiad.
A Challenge.

Named after the legendary Swiss mathematician Leonhard Euler, Euler100 is an online mathematics challenge for children under 13. It is deliberately designed to be welcoming, progressive, and globally accessible — not just for children who already excel, but for those who are discovering their potential.

Participants progress through 7 stages of increasing difficulty, supported every step by STELLA AI, our intelligent mathematics tutoring platform. The top 100 qualifiers worldwide earn an invitation to the inaugural finale in Disentis, Switzerland in October 2027.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Who can enter Euler100?
Any child who is under 13 years of age by 31 December 2027 may participate, regardless of nationality or school type. Registration requires either a parent/guardian's permission or a teacher's enrolment via a class code.
Do I need to be a maths genius to start?
Absolutely not. Stage 1 (Explorer) is designed for Grade 6–7 level mathematics. STELLA AI will tutor you at every step. Euler100 is for curious minds, not just top-of-class performers.
What happens to my child's personal data?
We take child safety and privacy extremely seriously. Children use an "Euler alias" (a pseudonym) on the platform — their real name is never displayed to other users. The platform is GDPR and COPPA compliant. No data is sold. Parent accounts have full visibility and control.
Is there a cost to participate?
Stages 1–3 are completely free. A one-time $10 USD payment unlocks Stages 4–7. There is also an optional $3/month STELLA AI Premium tier. Schools can access a bundle for ~$6 per student for groups of 10 or more.
What language is the challenge in?
The platform launches with English, French, Spanish, and German. Portuguese, Arabic, and Hindi will be added before the qualifying period opens in 2026.
