Child Safety

Designed for children.
Trusted by parents.

Every STELLA account for a child under 13 requires explicit consent from a parent or teacher before the learner can access any content. We use two routes: parental email consent, or teacher-managed class enrolment.

📧
Route A — Parent Consent Child registers, parent receives a consent email, parent approves. Account activates instantly.
🏫
Route B — Teacher Enrolment Teacher creates a class code. Children join the class. Teacher acts as responsible adult on the platform.
🎭
Euler Alias System Children choose a creative pseudonym — their "Euler alias". Real names are never shown to other users.
